# Privacy Policy 🔒

**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

**YouTube Playlist Watch Time Calculator** ("we", "our", or "the extension") is deeply committed to protecting your privacy and security. This policy transparently explains our data practices.

## 1. Zero Data Collection & Analytics
We firmly believe in data minimization. The extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage metrics, web requests, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), or telemetry. There are no tracking scripts or background servers monitoring your activity.

## 2. Explanation of Browser Permissions
The extension only requests the permissions strictly necessary to function:
- **`*://*.youtube.com/*`**: Required solely to inject the content scripts that read the public playlist duration timestamps directly from the YouTube DOM (HTML elements).
- **`storage`**: Necessary to remember your personal UI preferences (such as your active theme or language choice) locally across browser sessions.

## 3. Local Data Processing
- **Calculations:** All playlist calculations (parsing video timestamps and determining total duration/playback speed) happen natively within your own browser client. We have absolutely **zero access** to your private YouTube playlists, viewing history, account details, or browsing habits.
- **Settings:** Your extension configurations never leave your device. They are managed exclusively through your browser's local API (`chrome.storage.local` / `browser.storage.local`).

## 4. No Third-Party Sharing
Because we do not gather or log any user data whatsoever, it is impossible for us to share, sell, or rent data to any third parties, advertisers, or affiliated networks. 

## 5. Open Source Transparency
Our source code is fully open-source. Anyone (including privacy advocates and store reviewers) can independently audit the repository and verify that no hidden tracking, external requests, or malicious behavior exists within the codebase.

## 6. Official Disclaimer
This is an independent, community-driven project explicitly developed to improve user experience. It is **not** an official product of YouTube, Google LLC, Chrome Web Store, or Mozilla Firefox. We have **no corporate affiliation** with these entities.
